<interfaces>
<!-- Mobile audit patterns from research. -->

Tailwind 48px touch target patterns:
- Buttons/links with text: add `py-3` (12px + 12px + ~24px line height = ~48px) or `min-h-[48px]`
- Icon-only buttons (heart, close ×): add `p-3` (48px effective tap area around 24px icon)
- For inline links in nav: add `flex items-center min-h-[48px]`

No horizontal scroll fixes:
- Fixed-width containers: replace `w-96` or `w-[400px]` with `w-full max-w-sm` or similar
- Flex rows: add `flex-wrap` if items might overflow on narrow screens
- Tables: wrap in `overflow-x-auto`
- Modal max-width: `max-w-lg w-full` is fine (already has max-w, constrained to viewport)

Known mobile risk areas (from RESEARCH.md):
1. Navbar — on narrow screens the flex row with logo + links + auth section may overflow
2. ResultsPage match cards — grid may not wrap correctly at 375px
3. MatchDetailPage standings table — tables overflow on mobile
4. CreatePostModal — modal may not be scrollable on small screens
5. FanBasePage 4-column team grid — may need to reduce to 2 cols on mobile
</interfaces>
