## Milestone Summary

Football Finder v1.0 delivered a complete football match discovery platform — from a bare Express scaffold to a fully deployed product with match search, FanBase community pages, authentication, and launch polish. Built by a 2nd year CS student in 5 days (2026-03-15 → 2026-03-19).

## Key Accomplishments

1. **Match Discovery** — City + date range search across 10 European leagues (top 2 per country in England, Spain, Germany, Italy, France), full match detail pages with team stats, ticket links, and Google Maps navigation
2. **FanBase Browse** — Country → League → Team 3-step navigation, direct team search, tabbed post feeds — fully readable as a guest with no account required
3. **Auth System** — Password-based registration, 7-day httpOnly session cookies, upvote/edit/delete/favorites for full account users
4. **Post Creation** — All 4 post types live: General Tip, Seat Tip, Pub Recommendation, I'm Going (closed via Phase 6 gap)
5. **Launch Polish** — Transportation guide, contact form with email delivery, 404 page, mobile responsiveness (48px targets, no horizontal scroll)
6. **FanBase Seeded** — 28 posts across 7 major teams (Chelsea, Arsenal, Barcelona, Real Madrid, Bayern, PSG, Juventus)

## Tech Debt Carried to v2

- `backend/src/lib/token.helpers.ts` — orphaned file, exports `createVerificationToken` which is never called
- `POST /api/admin/sync` — unprotected dev endpoint (must lock down or remove before public launch)
- `CreatePostModal.tsx` line 229 — sends `teamName` field in POST body, silently discarded by backend
- **POST-08 photo upload** — explicitly deferred to v2 (photoUrl column exists in schema ready for future use)
