## Milestone Goal

Transform the Transportation Guide page into a searchable stadium directory. Every stadium gets its own page with an embedded map, transport data, and top FanBase community posts. Users can find any stadium via search or Country → League → Team browse, and reach it from match detail and FanBase team pages.

---

## Key Accomplishments

1. **Stadium DB + API** — Added `latitude`/`longitude` to Stadium model; built `/api/stadiums/search` and `/api/stadiums/:id` endpoints with 11 integration tests
2. **Stadium Guide Hub** — New `/stadiums` hub with debounced search + Country→League→Team browse; replaced Transportation Guide in navbar and bottom nav
3. **Stadium Detail Page** — Full stadium page: team identity, interactive Leaflet OSM map, transport sections, community posts
4. **Rich Transport Sections** — JSON fields for airport routes, travel times, budget breakdown, payment info; Community Tips linked to FanBase `?tab=getting-there`
5. **Canonical Page Structure** — Locked section order (Getting There → Travel Times → Budget → Payment → Pro Tips → Apps → Parking → Community Tips); airport transport folded into Getting There
